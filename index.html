Rae of Hope

A 501(c)(3) non-profit community-based organization designed to uplift our community by providing mental support and resources.

About Us

We strive to help save lives and bring hope to those affected by depression and other mental illnesses. We believe that by taking one simple action, we can each help influence massive collective change to support #MentalHealth4ALL.

Our Services

Our goal is to work as a community.

  • Provide caring, trained volunteers who offer comfort,
    encouragement, and resources
  • Community resource information
  • Spiritual guidance, upon request
  • Mental illness awareness
  • Able to work with all ages

You're Not Alone

Whether you have struggled with suicide yourself, or have lost a love one,
know you are not alone.
Hear about personal experiences from people in your local community whose lives have been impacted by suicide.

New survivors, we understand what you're living through.
As we move through unprecedented times, thousands seek support. Through our community forum, let’s help provide support after suicide and support to those experiencing depression to prevent suicide. Losing a loved one to suicide can be life-altering. It is helpful to understand what you and your loved ones are experiencing.

Support Our Cause

Take one simple action to support your mental health today. Youth continue to face unique mental health challenges and risk factors for suicide. Let people in your life know you’re a safe person to talk to about mental health, and actively listen and engage when someone comes to you for help.
